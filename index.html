About Us

Centreback Productions is a visual content making firm and the visual branch.

Our main aim is to develop contents that are going to make an impact on the audience and the industry as whole. Our vision is not only to come up with quality content but also to change the whole infrastructure of the industry with rich contents.

We will try to make the best use of our resources and give the people global contents that they would enjoy- because that is exactly what they deserve and we strive for.
